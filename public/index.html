Winter Events in Tulsa!


Welcome to our page, here you will find the various events
Tulsa has to offer during the holiday season!

Rhema

Enjoy the Christmas lights with synchronized musical trees and dancing snowflakes, plus the ultimate photo backdrop by the park bridge

At a glance

  • The Rhema campus is located on 71st (Kenosha) between 145th (Aspen) and 161st (Elm) in Broken Arrow, Oklahoma.
  • Highlights include a 50-figure nativity scene, Noah’s ark and animals, and a synchronized musical display.
  • rhemalights.org

JollyTown

December 16th and 22nd Jolly Town is open until 10pm. December 17th, 18th, 19th, 20th, 21st Jolly Town is open until 9pm.

At a glance

  • Located at Tulsa’s Guthrie Green, located at 111 E Reconciliation Way, Tulsa, OK 74103
  • Come watch holiday movies, interactive obsticle course, and kids can make their own orniment at the Jollytown Craft Shop!
  • Free admission along with Santa meet and greet!
